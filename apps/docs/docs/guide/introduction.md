# What is Reviewskits?

**Reviewskits** is an open-source, self-hosted alternative to platforms like Senja or Testimonial.to. It's designed specifically for developers who want full control over their data and complete freedom over the UI.

## Why use Reviewskits?

- **Self-Hosted**: Your reviews stay on your servers. No third-party data silos.
- **Headless**: We provide the backend and the data. You build the frontend exactly how you like it.
- **API-First**: Everything is accessible via a REST API. Integrate with any tool or language.
- **Official SDKs**: Ready-to-use, zero-dependency packages for Vue, Nuxt, React, and Next.js.
- **Customizable Forms**: Create beautiful, branded forms to collect testimonials effortlessly.

## The Core Concept

Unlike traditional review widgets that force a specific design on your website, Reviewskits is "headless." 

This means we don't give you a pre-built iframe or script tag. Instead, we give you **data**. You use our SDKs or API to fetch that data and render it using your own components, styles, and animations.

## Open Source & Community

Reviewskits is open-source (MIT). We believe that your customer proof is one of your most valuable assets, and you should own the infrastructure that powers it.
