# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making Reviewskits a welcoming and respectful project for everyone — regardless of experience level, background, nationality, or identity.

## Our Standards

**We expect:**
- Respectful and constructive communication
- Welcoming feedback, even when you disagree
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

**We do not tolerate:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Dismissing contributions because of who someone is
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any behavior that would make someone feel unwelcome

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other community channel related to Reviewskits.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it by opening a private issue or emailing the maintainers directly.

Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially. Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who violate these standards.
